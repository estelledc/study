            J   Analysis of hyper-parameters
J.1   Analysis of λ
To systematically investigate the influence of the hyper-
parameter in Eq. (4), we conduct experiments by evaluat-
ing eight distinct values of λ on the img2caption task. As
illustrated in Figure 18, our experimental results demon-
strate that the variation in λ exhibits only a marginal im-
pact on model performance. In alignment with the method-
ology adopted in Lumina-mGPT (Liu et al. 2024), we con-
sequently fix λ at 1e − 5 for all LLM training procedures.
